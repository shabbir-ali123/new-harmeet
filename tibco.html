Overview

TIBCO - Manager

Business Connect is an on-premise enterprise B2B platform that facilitates secure communication and transfer of messages and documents between different business partners and a central host. The application, initially built over 20 years ago, was in dire need of modernization to keep up with current technological standards and user expectations. I initiated this project alongside a team member and, after establishing a solid foundation, transitioned the project to my team in India for further development. My involvement spanned end-to-end across various phases, working closely with stakeholders, UX researchers, developers, and QA teams.

Project Goals

  • Technology Modernization: Transitioning from outdated technology to a modern stack, enhancing the application's performance, security, and scalability.

  • User-Centric Design: Creating an intuitive and user-friendly interface to streamline user tasks, reduce complexity, and minimize reliance on customer support.

  • Revenue Optimization: Improving the user experience to increase task completion rates, reduce operational costs, and drive higher business profits.

    Discovery Phase:

    Information Gathering and Stakeholder Discussions

  • Objective: To align the product vision with business goals, understand stakeholder expectations, and define success metrics.

  • Approach: Initiated discussions with the Product Manager (PM) and other stakeholders to gather detailed requirements and understand the existing challenges. The stakeholder interviews highlighted several user pain points and obstacles in task completion, providing critical insights for further exploration.

    • Key Business Goals Identified:

  • Technology Modernization: The current technology stack was obsolete, hindering performance and user satisfaction.

  • Intuitive Design: A need for a user-friendly interface that simplifies task execution, reduces support queries, and enhances the overall user experience.

  • Revenue Increase: By improving usability and performance, the goal was to reduce friction in task completion, leading to higher productivity and, subsequently, increased revenue.

    • Design Research

    Partnering with UX Research:

  • Revenue Increase: By improving usability and performance, the goal was to reduce friction in task completion, leading to higher productivity and, subsequently, increased revenue.

    • Analysis and Evaluation:

  • User Workshops: Conducted workshops with users to gather qualitative data on their experiences, including positive aspects, pain points, and expectations for future enhancements.

  • Heuristic Evaluation: Carried out a systematic inspection of the user interface, identifying usability issues based on established usability principles.

  • Competitor Analysis: Analyzed competitors' products to benchmark against industry standards and identify areas where Business Connect could improve.

    • Identified Pain Points:

  • Complex Installation Process: The installation process was cumbersome and time-consuming, creating a significant barrier to entry for users.

  • High Volume of Customer Service Calls: The application generated an average of 20-25 calls per day, with users seeking assistance in completing tasks like creating business agreements or setting up partner profiles.

  • Poor Performance: The legacy system was sluggish, leading to user frustration and inefficiency.

  • Dependence on Third-Party Tools: Users needed to rely on external tools to view error messages and logs, complicating the troubleshooting process.

    • User Persona

    Usability Testing and Success Criteria:

    I partnered with the Usability Team for low-fidelity testing with some of our existing clients/users to evaluate our designs. I have been part of the observer and there were a couple of iterations that happened during testing.

    Analysis and Evaluation Data Gathered:

  • We have defined a series of tasks and asked users to perform the tasks in a prototype.
  • I was observing the user while performing the task and asking them to read out their thoughts aloud while performing the task till completion to understand where they faced difficulties and how their overall experience was.
  • We did usability testing with 8 users and gathered all the information and their experience with the application by keenly observing them. This helped us to better understand the user and identify areas that needed improvement.
  • With continuous iteration processes of feedback correction and testing data, we finalized the product with our last successful usability testing of the final product.

    • Business Connect was launched in March 2020, and so far, the findings for the product are as follows:

    Success Criteria:

  • Performance of the application has improved significantly with the latest technology.
  • Calls related to user difficulties in accessing the application with the customer service have been reduced drastically by 18%, allowing customers to focus on assistance-needed problems.
  • No third-party tool is needed to decode error messages and logs.
  • It's too early to comment on the product's growth, but the target is at least a 35-40% increase by 2021.
  • We have reduced the production time and training time of the application as the latest technology and user experience design of the product were easy for the user to understand all tasks and complete them without obstacles.
